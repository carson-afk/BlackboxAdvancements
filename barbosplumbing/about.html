our story

A family business,
built on trust.

Barbo's Plumbing started in 2009 with one truck, a master plumber, and a very simple rule: tell people the truth and do the work you promised. Fifteen years and 2,400+ homes later, the rule still applies.

Two people.
Every job.

Dan Barbo is a WA master plumber with decades of South Sound field experience. His daughter Presley has been on job sites since age sixteen and now runs half the crew. It's still a two-truck outfit by design — when you call Barbo's, you get a Barbo.

No rotating subs. No call-center dispatch. No "your crew will be so-and-so, maybe." The same two plumbers you spoke to are the ones showing up at your door.
