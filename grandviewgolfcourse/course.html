The Course

18 holes. Par 72.
5,900 yards of challenge.

A walking-friendly layout with water in play on many holes. Unique among Pacific Northwest courses: the 12th green lands at the clubhouse, giving you a true 12-hole option without skipping any holes.

Course layout

Designed for every player.

Grandview plays from 5,264 yards (red) to 5,900 yards (white), with par 72 across 18 holes. The front 9 routes through cedars and opens into a signature par-5 7th at 516 yards. The back 9 introduces more water and tighter approach shots — a real test on holes 14 through 17.

Slope and rating: White 68.8 / 110 for men, 74.0 / 126 for women. Red tees rate 70.3 / 118 for women.

Pace of play is brisk. Walk or ride — both are welcome. Power carts, pull carts, and club rentals are available at the pro shop.
