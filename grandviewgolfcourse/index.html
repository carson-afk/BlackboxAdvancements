Welcome to Grandview

A quieter round of golf.
With teeth.

Grandview is an 18-hole course open to the public, seven days a week. Our 5,900-yard layout features Pacific Northwest scenery, an easy walking routing, and plenty of in-play water to keep every hole honest.

The course has a unique feature: the 12th green lands at the club house. So whether you're playing 9, 12, or a full 18, the round fits your day — not the other way around.

"We should consider the possibility of making 12 holes a standard round ... of course it would meet resistance, but it would make sense in people's lives.— Jack Nicklaus

Don't let the low green fees fool you. Grandview plays challenging and fair — and offers the best value in the Pacific Northwest.
