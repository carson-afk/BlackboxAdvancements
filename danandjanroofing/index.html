Why we started

He hated being forced to cut corners.

Daniel Harvey started in roofing over 20 years ago. He fell in love with the trade fast — but watched shop after shop tell crews to skip the underlayment on the back slope because “nobody will see it.”

After a lot of kitchen-table conversations with his partner Janice Courchaine, they decided the only way to do it right was to do it themselves. In July 2020 they filed the paperwork, bought the truck, and started answering their own phone.

Every roof since has the underlayment on the back slope.
