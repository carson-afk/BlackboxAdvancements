Roof Repair

A leak caught early is a repair. A leak ignored is a rebuild.

Missing shingles, failed flashing, storm damage, ventilation issues — we diagnose the real problem, then fix it honestly. We'll tell you when a repair makes sense and when it doesn't.

  • Storm, wind, and hail damage
  • Failed flashing around chimneys, vents, skylights
  • Missing, cracked, or curling shingles
  • Leak detection and interior water damage assessment
  • Ventilation and ice-dam correction

How it goes

From first call to final walk-through.

No surprises, no sales gymnastics. Here's exactly what happens when you hire us.

  1. 01
    Free estimate

    Dan walks the roof and gives you a straight-shooting assessment. No pressure, no fake urgency.

  2. 02
    Written proposal

  3. 03
