FAQ

Mowing, answered.

Do I need to be home when you mow?
Nope. Most of our clients aren't. As long as gates are unlocked, we handle it and send you a photo when we're done.
What if it rains on my mow day?
We reschedule to the next dry day, usually within 24–48 hours. You'll get a text when we're rolling your way.
Do you bag or mulch the clippings?
Mulching by default — it's better for your lawn. We'll bag if you prefer, just let us know.
How tall is too tall for a regular mow?
Over about 8" usually needs a one-time rescue cut first. Totally fine — we do those all the time.
