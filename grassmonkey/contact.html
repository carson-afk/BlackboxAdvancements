Meet Daniel

Daniel Harvey started Grass Monkey because he got tired of hearing the same complaint: "the lawn guy never showed up." He's a Spokane Valley local, a stickler for tidy edges, and the guy who will actually pick up your call.

Every customer gets his real cell, photo updates after every visit, and a handshake promise: if you don't love the work, he comes back and fixes it.

The $99 minimum is on purpose. It keeps even small jobs viable for a one-truck crew so we can stay friendly, fast, and reliable instead of cutting corners on quick visits.

Frequently asked

Quick answers.

Is there really a $99 minimum?
Yep — every visit, regardless of service. It keeps quick jobs worthwhile for a small crew so we can stay friendly and reliable. Bigger jobs scale up from there.
