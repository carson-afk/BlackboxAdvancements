Our story (short version)

Grass Monkey Property Maintenance launched in 2026 because there aren't enough lawn care companies that actually show up when they say they will. We're local, friendly, and obsessively tidy about edges.

Every customer gets our real phone number, photo updates after every visit, and a handshake promise: if you don't love the work, we come back and fix it.

Why the name? Because a monkey with a pair of shears is a lot more fun than a logo full of blades, and because we like making people smile when they drive home.
