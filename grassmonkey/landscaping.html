Fertilizing & Seeding

Feed it right. Fill the gaps.

A seasonal fertilizer plan — plus overseed on tired spots — is the fastest way to push your lawn from "fine" to "the best on the block." We use pet- and kid-friendly products and time each application to the Spokane Valley climate.

  • Spring, summer & fall applications
  • Slice-seed or broadcast overseed
  • Pet-safe & kid-safe products
  • Weed pressure dropped the natural way
Aerating & Dethatching

Open the soil. Change everything.

Spokane Valley soil compacts hard, and thatch chokes out water and fertilizer before it can reach the roots. Core aeration pulls plugs, dethatching rips up the dead mat — and suddenly your lawn actually absorbs what you're paying for.

  • Core aeration (pulls real plugs, not spikes)
  • Power dethatching with debris haul-off
  • Best paired with overseed & fertilizer
  • Timed for fall or early spring
Lawn Rescue Plan

Thin, patchy lawn? Give it a season.

If your lawn is thin, patchy, or overrun — we'll build a one-season recovery plan. Usually a dethatch + aerate + overseed + fertilizer combo, spaced out right. Most yards look noticeably better within 6 weeks.

  • Honest assessment (no upsell pressure)
  • Custom plan in writing
  • Progress photos at each visit
