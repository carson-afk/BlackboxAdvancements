About · American Air Heating & Cooling, LLC

Your final stop for
real solutions.

American Air Heating & Cooling (wallawallaair.com) is Dan's practice — and by design, it stays small. Every assessment, every commissioning, every homeowner walkthrough is owner-led, from the first visit to the final invoice. Precision. Punctuality. Excellence.

A trade practiced slowly.
Done properly.

Too many HVAC shops optimize for "jobs per day." American Air optimizes for one thing: the install should look — and feel — like the one in the manufacturer's reference photograph.

That means we take fewer projects, spend longer on each, and make sure the homeowner understands what we did and why. Precision. Punctuality. Excellence. isn't just a tagline — it's the standard we measure every job against.

Dan founded American Air Heating & Cooling because the Valley deserved better than what was being offered. Three core services — ductless mini-splits, complete HVAC, and gas fireplace work — plus a Preventative Protection Plan for members. That's the whole catalog. We say no to anything outside it.

Our commitments

What we always do.

I

Walk the job first

Dan personally performs every on-site assessment. Load calc, equipment check, duct layout review — before any proposal leaves our office.

II

Itemize the proposal

Equipment, labor, permit, commissioning, and applicable rebates — each broken out. You'll see every number before you sign.

III

Commission personally

Combustion analysis, refrigerant weighed in, static pressure measured, thermostat programmed. Dan signs off before you're billed.

Stand behind the work

10-year equipment warranty. 2-year workmanship guarantee. Issue arises later? We come back. No charge, no disagreement.
