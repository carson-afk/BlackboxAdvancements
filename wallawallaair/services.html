Service catalog

A narrow practice,
executed thoroughly.

We specialize rather than generalize. The services below are each owner-led by Dan, backed by 10-year equipment and 2-year workmanship guarantees.

I · Ductless

Ductless mini-splits.

Mitsubishi and Daikin heat pumps — single zone to whole-home multi-zone. Ideal for additions, historic homes, tasting rooms, and retrofits where duct work isn't feasible.

  • Single- and multi-zone systems
  • Wall, ceiling cassette, floor-mount
  • Wi-Fi / smart control
  • Clean line-hide routing
II · HVAC install

Complete HVAC retrofits.

Full system change-outs with proper Manual-J design, high-static duct work, and factory-quality mechanical-room aesthetics.

  • Load calculation first
  • Variable-speed + inverter options
  • Duct static measurement
  • Permit pulled, inspected
III · Fireplace

Gas fireplace install & service.

Modern linear, traditional cottage inserts, and outdoor gas appliances. We handle the fuel plumbing, venting, and annual servicing.

  • Direct-vent gas fireplaces
  • Inserts & conversions
  • Outdoor fire tables
  • Annual safety servicing
IV · Gas plumbing

Natural gas line work.

New runs for outdoor kitchens, pool heaters, standby generators, and appliance relocates. Pressure-tested and permitted — every time.

  • Appliance gas runs
  • Generator supply lines
  • Outdoor kitchen / BBQ lines
  • Pressure test + permit sign-off
V · Diagnostics

Service & repair.

Same-day diagnostics on failing systems. We catch faults others miss — and we explain what we find in plain language with written documentation.

  • Refrigerant + combustion analysis
  • Blower, motor, control-board replacement
  • Written condition report
  • No-upsell diagnostic policy
VI · Maintenance

Seasonal maintenance programs.

Two-visit annual programs: spring AC readiness, autumn furnace tune-up. Priority dispatch for members, discounts on repairs.

  • Coil clean + rinse
  • Combustion + refrigerant verify
  • Written condition report
  • Member priority dispatch
VII · IAQ

Indoor air quality.

Media filtration, UV germicidal, whole-home humidifiers, and HRV/ERV installs for homes that need better-than-standard air.

  • MERV 13–16 filtration
  • UV germicidal lamps
  • Whole-home humidity
  • HRV / ERV installs
VIII · Controls

Smart thermostats & zoning.

Ecobee, Nest, Honeywell — properly wired (C-wire included), configured, and walked through with the homeowner.

  • Zoning dampers
  • Smart thermostat install
  • Control-wiring audit
  • Homeowner walkthrough
IX · Commercial

Light commercial.

Tasting rooms, offices, small retail — HVAC service contracts and install projects with after-hours scheduling.

  • Tasting room climate control
  • Service contracts
  • After-hours schedule
  • Emergency response
