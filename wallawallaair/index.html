The practice

A careful catalog.
A complete one.

We select our projects carefully and see each through from assessment to commissioning. Every job is owner-led — Dan personally commissions every install before we invoice.

I · Ductless Mini-Splits

Zone heating & cooling,
done elegantly.

Mitsubishi and Daikin heat pumps for additions, historic homes, wine cellars, tasting rooms, and primary-residence retrofits. Clean line-hides, tidy condensate runs, inverter-driven efficiency.

II · HVAC Install

Complete system retrofits.

Full HVAC change-outs — design, permit, installation, and commissioning. Properly sized systems, high-static duct work, clean mechanical-room layouts.

III · Fireplaces

Hearth work & gas appliances.

Gas fireplace installs, conversions, and annual servicing. From minimalist linear units to traditional cottage inserts — we handle the gas plumbing and the venting.

IV · Natural Gas Lines

Safe, permitted gas plumbing.

Gas line installs for outdoor kitchens, pool heaters, standby generators, and appliance relocates. Pressure-tested, permitted, signed off clean.

V · Service & Repair

Diagnostics and
attentive follow-through.

Dan has a reputation for catching the issue three prior technicians missed. We trace faults to root cause and document everything for the homeowner.

VI · Seasonal Maintenance

Spring tune-up,
autumn readiness.

Two-visit annual programs for existing customers. Coil clean, refrigerant verify, combustion analysis, and a written condition report each visit.

Our method

A considered approach.

I

Assess

Dan performs the walk-through personally. Load calc, refrigerant check, duct static — the work a fast tech skips gets done here before we quote.

II

Design

Right-sized equipment, proper duct layout, sensible mechanical-room aesthetics. Manufacturer rebates and Energy Trust incentives itemized.

III

Install

Clean work, tidy refrigerant runs, code-compliant venting, color-coded low-voltage. The install should look like a factory photograph.

IV

Commission

Combustion analysis, refrigerant weighed in, static pressure recorded, thermostat programmed, homeowner walked through every control.
