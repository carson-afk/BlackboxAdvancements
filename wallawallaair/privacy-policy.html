Privacy Policy

Your information,
used only for your project.

American Air Heating & Cooling, LLC ("Walla Walla Air") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

When you request an estimate, call us, email, or submit a web form, we collect the information you provide: your name, property address, phone, email, and a description of your project. When you use our site, we also receive standard technical data (browser, device, general location, referring URL) through analytics.

How we use it

  • To respond to your request and quote your project.
  • To schedule, perform, and follow up on work at your address.
  • To send receipts, warranty registration, and service reminders.
  • If you finance with Wells Fargo Home Projects®, to submit the application you authorize.
  • To improve our site and understand which pages homeowners find useful.

How we don't use it

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with marketers or data brokers. We don't rent our customer list. If a third party ever contacts you claiming to be on our behalf, please tell us — it isn't us.

Who we share it with

Only the parties necessary to complete your project:

  • Equipment manufacturers — for warranty registration on the equipment we install in your home.
  • Wells Fargo Bank, N.A. — only if you choose to apply for Home Projects® financing through us, and only the application data you authorize.
  • Utility / rebate programs — only if you ask us to process a rebate (Energy Trust of Oregon, local PUD, etc.).
  • Our crew and service scheduling software — to dispatch the right technician to your address.

Cookies & analytics

We use basic analytics cookies to understand site usage (aggregate numbers — page views, device types, general region). We don't use advertising tracking pixels on our own site. You can disable cookies in your browser; our site will still work normally.

SMS / text messaging

If you opt in to text communication (by submitting the estimate form with a mobile number, or by replying to a confirmation text), we'll use SMS only for service scheduling and job-related updates. Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Data retention

We retain project records (invoices, warranty registrations, service notes) as long as we have an ongoing relationship with your equipment, plus the period required by Washington tax and warranty law. You can request deletion of any non-required data at any time by emailing office@wallawallaair.com.

Your rights

You may ask us what information we hold about you, correct it, or request deletion where we're not legally required to keep it. Email office@wallawallaair.com or call (509) 525-1339 and we'll respond within a reasonable time.

Children's privacy

Our services are for homeowners and commercial property owners. We don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we handle your information, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date below. The current version is always posted at this URL.
