Services · Indoor air quality

Indoor air quality.

Media filtration, UV germicidal lamps, whole-home humidifiers, and HRV/ERV installs for homes that need better-than-standard air — wildfire smoke season, allergies, tight new builds.

Best for

When this applies.

  • Households with allergies, asthma, or respiratory sensitivity
  • Homes that close up tight during wildfire smoke season
  • New construction where natural infiltration is too low
  • Anyone tired of dust accumulation and dry winter air
What's included

The full scope.

MERV 13–16 media filtration

Real filtration, not the 1-inch cardboard filter that came with the system.

UV germicidal lamps

Coil-mounted UV-C — kills mold and bacteria on the wet evaporator.

Whole-home humidification

Bypass or fan-powered humidifiers tied to a humidistat.

HRV / ERV installs

Heat / energy recovery ventilators for tight homes that need fresh-air exchange.

How we do it

Our process.

01

IAQ audit

We measure baseline humidity, particulate, and ventilation rate.

02

Solution design

Right-sized equipment for the actual air volume of your home.

03

Install + integrate

Tied into existing duct work, controls integrated with your thermostat.

04

Verify performance

Post-install measurement so you can see the change, not just trust it.
