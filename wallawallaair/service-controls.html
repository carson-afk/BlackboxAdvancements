Smart thermostats & zoning.

Ecobee, Nest, Honeywell — properly wired with C-wire, configured for your equipment, and walked through with the homeowner. Plus zoning damper systems for homes that need different temps in different areas.

Best for

When this applies.

  • Two-story homes where upstairs/downstairs comfort doesn't match
  • Homeowners ready to upgrade from a basic round dial
  • Smart-home setups that need climate integration
  • Anyone replacing a thermostat that lost wiring documentation
