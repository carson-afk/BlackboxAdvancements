Services · Gas fireplace

Gas fireplace install & service.

Modern linear, traditional cottage inserts, and outdoor gas appliances. We handle the fuel plumbing, venting, and annual servicing — one vendor for the whole hearth.

Best for

When this applies.

  • New fireplace installs in remodels or new builds
  • Replacing wood-burning fireboxes with cleaner gas inserts
  • Outdoor gas appliances — fire tables, patio heaters, BBQ
  • Annual safety servicing on existing gas units
What's included

The full scope.

Direct-vent gas fireplaces

Modern linear and traditional models — Heat & Glo, Mendota, Regency.

Inserts & conversions

Drop-in inserts that turn an inefficient firebox into clean, controllable heat.

Outdoor gas appliances

Fire tables, patio heaters, and BBQ rough-ins with permitted gas runs.

Annual safety servicing

Burner clean, glass clean, ignition test, gas-pressure verification.

Our process.

02
