OUR STORY

FAMILY-OWNED.
SALEM-BUILT.

Wolf Pack Plumbing was started in 2023 by Joe — a journeyman plumber who got sick of watching neighbors get mystery-priced, upsold, and ghosted by the big franchise shops. The idea was simple: show up fast, quote flat, stand behind the work, pick up the phone.

Three years in, we run the same way. A real human answers. You see the price before the tools come out. Every install is warrantied six years end-to-end. Every drain we clear is backed by a 30-day return-for-free promise. That's the deal.
