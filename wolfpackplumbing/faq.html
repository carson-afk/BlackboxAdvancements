QUESTIONS / ANSWERS

STUFF PEOPLE
ACTUALLY ASK.

The questions Joe takes on the phone, plus the ones people wish they'd asked before hiring their last plumber. If yours isn't here, call or text — we'll answer it for real.

Are you licensed, bonded, and insured?+
Yes. Oregon CCB #244621, fully bonded, and carrying general liability + workers' comp. Any Salem-area plumber should be able to recite their CCB on demand — if they can't, call us instead.
How does your pricing actually work?+
Flat-rate, every time. We walk the job, pull a number (usually good/better/best options), and you see the price before a single tool comes out of the truck. No hourly meters, no "well the part ended up being more" invoice surprises. If the job opens up smaller than scoped, the price comes down.
What's the 6-year install warranty cover?+
Everything we install — water heater, main line, fixture, valve — is covered for six years, parts and labor. No fine-print exclusions. If our install fails in year five, we come fix it on our dime.
What's the 30-day no-clog guarantee?+
If we clear a drain and the same drain backs up within 30 days, we come back free. That's the promise. It keeps us honest about diagnosing the real problem (roots, scale, cracked pipe) instead of just snaking the clog and leaving.
Do you really answer at 2am?+
Yep. Dispatch is 24/7. A real person — not a call center, not a voicemail — picks up. Average response across Salem/Keizer/Woodburn is about 35 minutes.
What's the first thing I should do in a plumbing emergency?+
Shut off the water at the main valve — usually on the street-facing side of the house, in a box near the meter, or in the garage / crawlspace. If you're not sure, call us first; we'll walk you through finding it on the phone while we dispatch a truck.
Do you offer financing?+
Yes — we partner with Wisetack. Soft credit check (doesn't ding your score), instant approval, and flexible monthly terms. Great for water heaters, repipes, or bigger remodel plumbing. Ask when you call.
Do you work with property managers and landlords?+
Heavily. We do turnover inspections, scheduled preventative maintenance, tenant-friendly dispatch, and portfolio-level billing. Direct line to Joe for PM accounts — no ticket queues.
What areas do you cover?+
Primary coverage: Salem, Keizer, Woodburn, Silverton, Stayton, Turner, Monmouth, Dallas, Independence, Mt. Angel. Outside that, we still come — a small travel fee shows up on the estimate so you're not surprised. See the areas page for each town's notes.
Do you handle remodels and new construction plumbing?+
Yes. Rough-in and finish plumbing, tub-to-shower conversions, vanity / shower-valve relocates, full kitchen & bath rough packages. We coordinate with your GC (or ours) and pull permits. See services.
Can I get a rough price before you come out?+
Absolutely — run the estimator. It takes about 60 seconds and gives you a real range pulled from six years of Valley job data. Final number locks at the on-site walk-through.
Do you offer a new-customer discount?+
We do — ask when you call. It's not plastered in big numbers because it's not a gimmick, it's a thanks-for-trying-us. Mention it on the phone.
