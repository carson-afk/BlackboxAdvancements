Services · 24/7 Emergency

24/7 emergency HVAC
for the Valley.

No heat in a snap, no cool in a heatwave, gas smell in the basement — when the system fails outside business hours, call the same line: (509) 525-1339. Dispatch answers, and a technician is on the road.

We roll on

What counts as an emergency.

  • No heat in winter or no cool in a heatwave
  • Smell of natural gas (leave the house first — then call)
  • Active refrigerant or water leak from an indoor unit
  • CO alarm going off from a combustion appliance
  • Commercial tasting-room or restaurant climate-control failure
