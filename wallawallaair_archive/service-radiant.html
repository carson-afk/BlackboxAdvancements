Services · Radiant & hydronic

Radiant heat & hydronic systems.

Warm floors, even heat, zero blowing air. We design, install, and service in-floor radiant and hydronic systems — additions, new builds, and remodels — paired with the right boiler or heat-pump hydronic source.

Best for

When this applies.

  • Slab-on-grade new builds and additions
  • Remodels where you want warm tile, stone, or hardwood floors
  • Historic homes where forced-air is disruptive or impossible
  • Shop, garage, and out-building heat without blowing dust
What's included

The full scope.

In-floor radiant

PEX tubing, poured over or tacked under subfloor, zoned by manifold.

Hydronic baseboard & panel

When you want quiet radiant comfort without the floor work.

Boilers & hydronic heat pumps

Navien, Viessmann, and air-to-water heat pumps sized to the load.

Controls & zoning

Thermostats, zone valves, mixing manifolds — programmed for real use, not a spec sheet.

How we do it

Our process.

01

Design the loops

Room-by-room loop layout & flow calculations before anything gets poured or stapled.

02

Pick the heat source

Condensing gas boiler or air-to-water heat pump depending on budget and efficiency target.

03

Install & pressure-test

PEX run, manifold set, pressure held before anyone finishes over the top.

04

Commission & balance

Flow rates set, thermostats paired, zone balance verified room-by-room.
