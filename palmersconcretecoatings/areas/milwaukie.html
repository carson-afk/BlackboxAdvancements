Why Milwaukie homes choose us.

Small Milwaukie garages turn out beautifully with light-color solid or flake systems that bounce light around the space.

Every Milwaukie project gets the same four-step system: diamond grinding, full crack and spall repair, high-build base coat with decorative flake or quartz, and a UV-stable polyaspartic top coat. We do not cut corners. We do not acid-etch. We do not use roll-on kits.
