Four steps.
No surprises.

Diamond grind. Repair. Coat. Cure.
Done in 1-3 days for most homes.
01

Free Walkthrough

We measure the space, check the slab condition, talk colors and finishes, and email a line-item quote within 48 hours.

02

Diamond Grind & Repair

Industrial diamond grinders open the concrete profile. Cracks, pits, and spalling get filled with structural epoxy.

03

Coat & Broadcast

High-build epoxy or polyaspartic base coat goes down. Decorative flake or quartz broadcast to the desired coverage.

04

Top Coat & Walkthrough

UV-stable polyaspartic top coat seals the floor. Walk on it the next day, drive on it in 24-48 hours.
