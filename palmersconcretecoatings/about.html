Built on referrals.

Palmer's Concrete Coatings has been putting down floors across Oregon for over 14 years. We do not advertise on billboards and we do not do high-pressure sales. Most of our work comes from word of mouth.

Who we are.

We are a family-run concrete coating contractor based in Boring, Oregon. We specialize in garage floor epoxy, polyaspartic systems, concrete staining, coloring, and sealing across the Portland metro.

Over the last 14+ years we have coated floors in more than 850 homes, shops, and commercial buildings from Sandy to Portland. We are licensed, bonded, and insured in Oregon (CCB # 250294), and we stand behind every floor we put down.

How we are different.

One crew, start to finish. You do not get passed off to subs or day-labor. The same team that gives you the estimate does the grinding, the repair, and the coating.

We grind, we do not acid-etch. Every floor starts with an industrial diamond grinder that opens the concrete profile mechanically. That is the only way to get a coating that actually bonds for 10+ years.

Honest pricing. No bait-and-switch. The number we give you in the quote is the number you pay, unless we hit a slab condition we could not see without the grinder.

Real warranties. Our residential systems carry a workmanship warranty. We come back and fix anything that is on us.
