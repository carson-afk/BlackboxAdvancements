Concrete Coloring

Concrete coloring covers everything from a solid-color epoxy floor to a custom multi-color decorative finish. If you have a specific shade or pattern in mind, we can match it or build it.

Solid color

Any color in any epoxy or polyaspartic. Pick from our deck or bring us a sample.

Broadcast quartz

Colored quartz broadcast into the base coat for a textured, slip-resistant finish in any blend of colors.

Multi-color accents

Borders, logos, parking lines, directional patterns: whatever the space needs.

Typical price range

$3 to $6 per square foot depending on complexity.
