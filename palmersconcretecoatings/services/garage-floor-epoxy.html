Garage Floor Epoxy

Our most requested service. Your garage floor takes more abuse than any other surface in your house - hot tires, oil, road salt, dropped tools, kids bikes, and Oregon winter moisture. A proper garage floor epoxy system from Palmer's gives you a floor that shrugs all of that off for a decade or more.

What is included

Diamond grinding to open the concrete profile. Crack and spall repair with structural epoxy. High-build epoxy base coat. Decorative flake broadcast (or quartz) in the color blend of your choice. UV-stable polyaspartic top coat.

Typical timeline

1 to 2 days for most 2-car garages. Walk on it the next day. Drive on it in 24 to 48 hours.

Typical price range

Most 2-car garages land in the $2,500 to $4,500 range. Use the estimator to get a wide ballpark for your specific floor.

Why not a DIY kit?

Big-box store kits are roll-on epoxies that skip the grinding step and use thinner material. They peel within a year or two. Our systems go down at 8-12x the thickness and bond mechanically to the concrete.
