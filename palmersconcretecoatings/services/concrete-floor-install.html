Concrete Install & Coating

We handle the whole slab, not just the top coat. Cracked, uneven, or damaged concrete gets repaired or replaced before a single drop of epoxy goes down. For new construction we pour, finish, and coat: one crew, one quote.

Repair services

Self-leveling overlay for uneven or pitted slabs. Structural epoxy for cracks and spalling. Polymer-modified concrete patches. Full slab replacement where needed.

New installation

New concrete pours for garages, shops, basements, and patios. Broom, trowel, or polished finish, then coated.

Why fix before coating

Epoxy locks in whatever is underneath. Coating a cracked or moisture-compromised slab without fixing it first is a waste of money. We test for moisture and evaluate every slab before we give a number.

Typical price range

$7 to $14 per square foot for repair + coating. New pours quoted per project.
