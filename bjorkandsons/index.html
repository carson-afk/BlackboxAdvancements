What we do

A narrow trade,
done thoroughly.

We pick fewer jobs and finish them properly — rather than racing between appointments. If we take your job, we'll be the only crew on it.

01 · Water heaters

Tank, tankless, and heat-pump.

Careful install, properly vented and strapped. Rheem, Bradford White, Rinnai, Navien. 10-year manufacturer warranty, 5-year workmanship.

02 · Leak & repair

Acoustic leak finding, minimal demolition.

Running toilets, shower valves, failing angle stops, slab leaks. We fix the actual problem — not the quick patch that fails in six months.

03 · Remodel plumbing

Kitchens and baths, rough-in to finish.

Permitted and inspected. Coordinated with your GC or ours. Clean venting, code-compliant DWV layout, tidy supply lines.

04 · Water line replace

From curb to house, properly.

Failing galvanized or polybutylene service lines replaced with PEX or copper. Trenchless available on qualifying runs.

05 · Sump & drainage

For the long wet winters.

Primary + battery backup sump installs for PNW basements and crawlspaces that can't afford to flood.

06 · Inspection

Pre-purchase or pre-winter.

Full-home plumbing inspection with camera on drains and visual on supply. Written report, photos, plain English.

How we work

Four guiding principles.

01

Quiet and considered

We don't show up with a marketing pitch. We show up, listen, and scope the problem — then talk about fixing it.

02

One job at a time

Your install gets our full attention from arrival to cleanup. No jumping between houses to close more tickets.

03

Honest ranges

Flat-rate pricing after walk-through. If the job opens smaller than expected, you pay less — not more.

04

We come back

5-year workmanship guarantee. Issue surfaces later? We return and make it right. No charge. Simple.
