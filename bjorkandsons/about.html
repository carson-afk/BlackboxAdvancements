About

A quiet trade,
passed down.

Kevin Bjork started repairing plumbing out of his own garage in 1985 — a Swedish-American kid with a wrench, a truck, and one goal: do the work well enough that people call back.

Forty years,
one standard.

Bjork & Sons Plumbing is a two-plumber outfit by design. Kevin still runs every job personally, usually with one of his sons alongside him. It's why we take fewer jobs each year than most shops book in a month — and why our customers stay customers.

We don't advertise much. We don't chase the call-center leads. What you get instead is a careful walk-through, an honest flat-rate quote, and the same two faces showing up to do the work. It's a small trade, run quietly — which is exactly how we like it.
