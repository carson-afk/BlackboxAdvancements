Services

A narrow trade,
handled thoroughly.

We don't chase every plumbing job in the phone book — we pick fewer and do them properly. Here's the full scope of what Bjork & Sons runs across the South Willamette.

01 · Heaters

Water heater install & repair.

Tank, tankless, heat-pump. Rheem, Bradford White, Rinnai, Navien, and more. Same-day diagnosis, manufacturer-backed installs.

  • Seismic strap, code venting
  • Drain pan & emergency shut-off
  • Old unit haul-away
  • 10-yr mfr + 5-yr workmanship
02 · Repairs

Fixture & leak repair.

Running toilets, failing shower cartridges, angle stops, disposals, slab leaks. Flat-rate after diagnosis.

  • Acoustic leak locating
  • Minimal drywall disruption
  • Real repair, not a patch
  • No-upsell policy
03 · Bath remodel

Bath remodel plumbing.

Rough-in and finish plumbing coordinated with your GC. Permitted, inspected, clean finish.

  • Tub-to-shower conversion
  • Vanity relocate
  • DWV layout & venting
  • Fixture installation
04 · Kitchen remodel

Kitchen rough-in.

Sink drops, DW/ice-maker lines, pot fillers, filtration. Clean, code-compliant, ready for the cabinet install.

  • Sink relocate
  • Instant-hot lines
  • Filtration plumbing
  • DW & disposal install
05 · Water lines

Service line replace.

Failing galvanized or polybutylene service lines swapped for PEX or copper. Trenchless on qualifying runs.

  • Curb-to-house
  • Trenchless pull
  • Permit handled
  • Utility coordination
06 · Sump & drainage

Sump pump service.

Primary + battery backup pumps for long PNW winters. Pit excavation, liners, and float switch diagnostics.

  • Primary + backup
  • Pit excavation
  • Perimeter drainage
  • Crawlspace dewatering
07 · Drains

Drain & sewer.

Stubborn clogs, camera inspection, hydro-jetting, root intrusion clearing, and spot sewer repair.

  • Snake + auger
  • Camera inspection
  • Hydro-jetting
  • Spot repair + reroute
08 · Emergencies

Emergency plumbing.

Burst lines, no hot water, overflowing fixtures. We prioritize existing customers but respond to new calls too.

  • Main shut-off coaching
  • Same-day stabilize
  • Flat-rate on arrival
  • Return for permanent fix
09 · Inspections

Pre-purchase inspection.

Full home plumbing inspection for buyers or pre-winter audits. Written report with photos.

  • Camera on drains
  • Supply pressure test
  • Water heater condition
  • Shut-off & PRV check
